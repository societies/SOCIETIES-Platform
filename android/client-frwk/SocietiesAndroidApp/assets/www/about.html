SOCIETIES

SOCIETIES ENABLES DISCOVERING, CONNECTING & ORGANISING OF RELEVANT PEOPLE, RESOURCES & THINGS, CROSSING THE BOUNDARY BETWEEN THE DIGITAL & PHYSICAL WORLD.

This Android application provides the user with features to manage their SOCIETIES experience and interact with other users and services on the SOCIETIES platform. The features include discovering relevant friends based on social network connections and context situations, joining communities to share information and services while having full control over your privacy preferences, and managing 3rd party Android applications and cloud services that build on the SOCIETIES platform
